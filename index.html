JEE (Joint Entrance Examination) is a national-level engineering entrance exam in India. It has two stages: JEE Main for NITs/IIITs and JEE Advanced for IITs. The exam tests Physics, Chemistry, and Maths and is highly competitive..

*CET (Common Entrance Test)* is a state-level exam in India for admission to engineering, pharmacy, and other professional courses. Each state (like Maharashtra with MHT-CET) conducts its own CET for local colleges. It tests subjects like *Physics, Chemistry, Maths/Biology* depending on the course..

NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) is a national-level exam in India for admission to MBBS, BDS, and other medical courses. It is conducted by the NTA and covers Physics, Chemistry, and Biology..

Competitive exams are tests conducted to select candidates for education or jobs based on merit. They often assess aptitude, subject knowledge, and reasoning skills..

E-learning is a method of learning using digital platforms and the internet, allowing access to educational content anytime, anywhere. It includes online courses, videos, virtual classrooms, and interactive tools..
